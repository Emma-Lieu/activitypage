We chose this design because........

Activity page

This page could have a different layout and color design than the other pages, therefor we chose a nature inspired color pallette, with browns and greens, this is because it fit the hero image we had in mind. However the typical brown and green will look washed out or blurry for many people with colorblindness, however magenta tones and green are color blindness friendly, we therefor chose a magenta undertoned brown for our pallette, which made it more colorblindness friendly. We also chose to have root colors in our css to have more consistancy with the color palette throughout the page, so it the page is cohesive. Furthermore, we chose to have a sticky navigation bar, so the users easily can click around the webpage for an easier scrolling experience. The " Pages " dropdown button also underlines what page the user is currently on, for better universal design. In addition to that the boxes have a shadow around them to emphasize where the Information lies for better readibility. We also chose to use " Arial Sans-Serif " font because it has the best readibility, so it takes in account for older people, or people with bad vision.
